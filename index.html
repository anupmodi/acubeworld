About Us

Our latest posts and rants.

ACube Worldwide was founded in 2017 by four professionals who had returned to India after having lived decades in USA. All returned for a common reason to be closer to their family and roots.

In the States, we all were working in the corporate sector, happy with our well-paying expat jobs. There was always a lingering thought to return to India someday, and start our own company – but the lure of the convenient lifestyle the corporate paycheck brings always over shadowed the heart! In 2011, we all finally mustered up the courage and quit our jobs and relocated to India. We had no clue what we were going to do and how we were going to do it -but our conviction was strong. After having lived two decades across USA, and having diverse work experience and our individual strengths in Finance, Operations, Technology and Marketing and in various fields from consulting to project management to product sourcing and marketing – we all knew that we could use our skill sets and create something wonderful with our experience in the ever-growing India and this Global marketplace! We four got together and started ACube worldwide to use the power of technology, process and internet to facilitate transparency in sourcing, design and production for our customers. A highly specialized product consulting, designing, sourcing and buying house. The current market lacks specialists who can assist in sourcing from India. We are small but we specialize! We understand what an international buyer (who may not speak our language, or may have never visited the country) is skeptical about doing business for the first time with a stranger. We help you to overcome that hurdle. We have lived overseas, worked in corporates and understand the importance of timelines, deadlines for deliveries, quality assurance, standards, ethics and most importantly we connect. We are not here just to develop a business relationship, we are here in business to build a business and a personal relationship of trust, integrity, longevity and understanding with our partners and clients. We only work with factories that practice fair trade; who understand the importance of timelines, who have ethical practices, ISO certified, and we try and bring you the best vendors to create your products – whether it’s with chemical free dye printed fabrics or handmade products or with other norms and regulations. We facilitate all certifications as per your requirement, become your representative in India, liaison with the vendors and make sure that both parties mutually benefit with the rates being offered and understand and deliver on each other’s requirements, get the quality checks done, get the payments processed and ensure timely delivery to you. We help small and midsize boutiques to procure products on competitive rates with a lower MOQ. For the larger format stores, we help you to get the best rates possible from factories that have been exporting for decades and specialize in their field.

We work with Manufacturers and artisan groups. Our speciality is in Textiles & Garments Handmade Paper Products Home Textiles & Furnishings Bedding Carpets Kitchen & Dining Textile Products Blue Pottery artisanal products Recycled & Upcycled Products Baby & Kids Wear Imitation Jewelry Fabric Toys Furniture Cement Bags Technology Exports and Facilitation We are today one of the well-known Indian buying house from Jaipur exporting and supplying a wide variety of products. We have expertise for sourcing, sampling, quality checks, Inspections, Audit and shipment co-ordination. With our strong business and personal relations with the country’s best suppliers, exporters and manufacturers dealing in a vast variety of products we bring the choicest range of products to our buyers offering them the best of designs, quality and prices. We support manufacturers who have strong eco-friendly policies with minimal waste (zero-wastage). They must practice fair trade, no child-labour engagement,recycling and upcycling and other ethical practices. We pride in being the newest (probably), professional and process driven buying office in India specialized in Purchasing and Sourcing.

Why should you work with us? We can be your dedicated sourcing and buyers in India and we will save you the cost and time of traveling here personally to get your work done. Your outsourced virtual India buying office and your local representative. We can also help you to launch your brand in India and sell your products in India online. We are responsive and we meet our delivery deadlines 99% of the time. We take up each task as a Project and execute it like big corporates do. We are working not just for business but for a deeper purpose of bringing together local exporters, artisans, manufacturers and international buyers so that both can benefit and creative an ethical and transparent way of doing business We aim to create a community of bringing Indian artisans and cottage industry manufacturers to the global map and help them increase their potential and creativity by working with international buyers and designers to create unique products.
