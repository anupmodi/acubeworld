About Us

How did ACube Enterprises LLP come about?

ACube Worldwide was founded in 2017 by professionals who left behind their flourishing corporate careers and returned to India.

While in the States, there was always a lingering thought to return to India someday, to go on our own instincts, to start our own company!

After having lived two decades across USA, and having diverse work experience and our individual strengths in Finance, Operations, Technology and Marketing and in various fields from consulting to project management to product sourcing and marketing – we all knew that we could use our skill sets and create something wonderful with our experience in the ever-growing India and this Global marketplace! So in 2011, we all finally mustered up the courage and quit our jobs and relocated to India with a strong conviction to put to test everything we had learnt in our prior lives in creating a specialty trading home.

We found that market lacks specialists who can assist in sourcing from India and thus we got together and started ACube worldwide to use the power of technology, and process rigor to facilitate transparency and specialization in product consulting, designing, sourcing and purchasing.

What is our Vision?

We specialize in bringing unique products to the world! We strive to build a business and a personal relationship based on core values of trust, integrity, longevity and understanding with our partners and clients.

Our Ethos

We work with factories practicing Fairtrade. Fair trade factories pay fair wages to their employees, and also create long-term, direct trading relationships based on dialogue, transparency, equity and respect. Fair trade uses a fair system of exchange to empower producers to develop their own businesses and to foster sustainable development. We follow a set of internationally-accepted fair trade principles and practices that are designed to improve the livelihood of low-income people through alternative trade. They are:

  • Transparency and long-term relationships
  • Commitment to fair pay for labor
  • Capacity building for long-term impact
  • Equal opportunity for women
  • Concern for the environment
  • Respect for cultural identity
  • Reasonable working conditions
  • No child exploitation

Our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative

Women empowerment

We work with several Not-For-profit organization that employ, empower and generate skills development for women in rural areas. Our fabric toys, handmade jewelry, hand painted scarves are made by women of these organizations. We organize training camps for them as required and bring their craftsmanship to the world by offering it to our international buyers and bringing them to a global platform of equal opportunity. As their marketing partner, we ensure they get their fair pay as requested by them for their products and their labor. In this, we allow our buyers to also contribute to the upliftment of the society in their own small way.

We work with specialized affiliates of ACCESS Development Services with an aim to link small producers to the mainstream markets. We provide sustainable livelihoods to small and primary producers through a network that facilitates access to mainstream markets with business intelligence and design support. Our product offering from these organizations includes apparels and accessories ranging from handcrafted jewelry of Jaipur to stoles and dupattas from varying crafts of block printing, tie 'n' dye, weaving amongst others; hand stitched leather and cloth bags and footwear. The home decor includes soft furnishings and decorative items in pottery and metal crafts.

All these products are produced by women members of Mahila Sansthas from the Indian states of Rajasthan, Uttrakhand, Bihar and Himachal Pradesh.

Zero-Waste and upcycling

We encourage all our buyers to sign up for zero wastage in their production schedules. In an effort to avoid harming the planet. We work with artisans and factories to ensure that every left over material from production is reused in making some of the products such as soft toys, handcrafted fabric jewelry or carry bags and hair accessories.

We design and create new products from remnant materials so they don’t end up in landfills. Creativity is key in upcycling. Our designers work side by side with the production team to upcycle and incorporate even the tiniest scraps into something beautiful. Our excess fabric strips are tediously hand cut and individually sewn back into making patchwork fabric.

We aim to reduce the carbon footprint from our production to 2-3%, which is pretty good, considering a typical factory can average 35-40%.

We also work with artisans who use leftover wasted fabrics and recycle it to make recycled fabric paper, which combines tiny scraps of fabric and paper.

Azo-Free Printing

The largest class of compounds used in dyestuffs are azo compounds and a few of thee dyes have been found to be potential carcinogens. These were first regulated in the EU by Germany and the list is commonly called the German banned dye list. If a product has not been made using these compounds or has a low component of Azo, it is called azo-free. It is usually hard to get 100% azo-free prints in block printing process but it can be achieved in screen printing. We work with printers who specialize in azo-free printing and thus keep our production process chemical-free and safe.

Low Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)

We realized that there was a gap in the market. There were big buyers like format stores who went to the big factories and required 10-20000 pieces in production. However, these factories did not entertain small entrepreneurs and buyers, who were left in the lurch to either buy at retail prices and resell at high prices in their home country or order high quantities and be left with a lot of dead-stock. IN order to address this, we started our company to provide solutions to the small buyer who wants low quantities, maybe just experiment with certain products; small boutique buyers, entrepreneurs. For us it’s not just business, it’s about building a long lasting bond and relationship and to be a part of our buyers and partners journey of growth , We see ourselves as facilitators and enablers for the global buyer who wants a piece of India.
