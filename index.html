Mission

What is our Mission?

ACube Worldwide is a trading home for both international and domestic import and export activity. Main activities undertaken by the Company include direct and indirect exports, imports of various goods and merchandise for and from around the world. We have a customer base of over 100 from around the world, specifically in regions such as North & South America, Europe, South East Asia & Australia with a vendor base for various products over 100 suppliers.

We specialize in the export of:

  • Textiles – apparel and accessories (adults and kids)
  • Home furnishings
  • Arts & handicrafts
  • Handmade paper products
  • Jewelry
  • Technology

ACube Enterprises is in the business of Exporting merchandise from India. The Company is engaged in exports of various items like textiles, Home products, Baby & Nursery clothing and Bedding; Scarves & Stoles, Bedding & Bath; Handicraft products; Handmade Paper products; Brass items.

About Us

How did ACube Enterprises LLP come about?

ACube Worldwide was founded in 2017 by four professionals who left behind their flourishing corporate careers and returned to India.

While in the States, there was always a lingering thought to return to India someday, to go on our own instincts, to start our own company! After having lived two decades across USA, and having diverse work experience and our individual strengths in Finance, Operations, Technology and Marketing and in various fields from consulting to project management to product sourcing and marketing – we all knew that we could use our skill sets and create something wonderful with our experience in the ever-growing India and this Global marketplace! So in 2011, we all finally mustered up the courage and quit our jobs and relocated to India with a strong conviction to put to test everything we had learnt in our prior lives in creating a specialty trading home.

We found that market lacks specialists who can assist in sourcing from India and thus we four got together and started ACube worldwide to use the power of technology, and process rigor to facilitate transparency and specialization in product consulting, designing, sourcing and purchasing.
